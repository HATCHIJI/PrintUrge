Frequently Asked Questions

Need a quick answer before placing a print request? Start here.

Do I need an account to print?

No. You can submit print requests directly. Logging in helps if you want easier tracking and repeat orders.

Is this only for students?

Students are the main audience, but the platform is open to anyone who needs printing services.

Can I request rush printing?

Yes. Rush slots are available based on partner shop capacity and selected service type.

What file formats are supported?

Common formats like PDF, DOCX, PPT, PNG, and JPG are supported for most service types.
